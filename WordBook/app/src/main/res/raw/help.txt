<h1>Summary</h1>
It is an application that combines function of the words book and the word card.<br/>
In the word card mode, the word card can automatically to turn to the page of meaning from the page of a word.
Time until the turn is can be set in increments of 1 second to 1 second to 60 seconds.
Settings , please go from the menu settings.
